Registration

Registration is required for all contestants entering Championship, International, and Junior divisions. Spectators may attend the event for free.

Registration involves two steps:

  1. Paying registration fee
  2. Uploading music

These two steps can be completed separately any time before the contest, but please keep the following deadlines in mind. After each deadline is past, the registration fee will increase.

Early bird deadlineSunday June 22
Regular deadlineSunday June 29
Final deadlineFriday July 4
No fees or music will be accepted on July 5

Step 1: Registration fee

Please review the contest's format and your eligibility carefully, and select the division(s) you'd like to enter. Then click the "Fee payment" button which will bring you to the payment processing page.

  • You can have someone else (parent, friend, etc) pay for you. In step 2 below, you will enter the actual contestant's information.
  • Payment is processed by PayPal, but you can pay as a guest using a credit card without a PayPal account.
  • If this does not work, please contact the organizer for alternatives.
  🇨🇦 Championship 1A$45
  🇨🇦 Championship Open   $45
  International   $45
  Junior$35
Total (CAD)$0

Step 2: Music upload

In this step you will enter your contestant information and upload the music for your routine(s).

You can upload or change your music as many time as you like before Sunday June 29. After that, a late fee will apply.

Upload music

That's it!
